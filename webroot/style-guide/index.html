Another Big Ticket Rental Conversion

Terry had been renting a Barko processor from a dealer in Kamloops to keep his operation moving, but the monthly rental costs were adding up with nothing to show for it long term. We stepped in and worked with one of our lending partners to structure a commercial lease-to-own solution, applying his accumulated rental credits in place of a traditional down payment.

The result was lower monthly payments than he had been paying to rent, full payout to the dealership, and a clear path to ownership of the machine.
